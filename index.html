The P-O-C-E Framework for Precision Prompting

Use this framework to generate highly focused and effective prompts for any AI model. **Clarity drives better results.**

P: Persona/Role

Define the **persona** or **expert role** the AI should adopt. This sets the context, tone, and knowledge base for the response.

O: Objective/Task

State the single, clear, and action-oriented **objective** using a strong verb. What do you need the AI to *do*?

C: Context/Constraints

Provide all necessary **context, rules, limits, and style instructions**. This is where you include regulatory requirements, word counts, or complexity levels.

E: Example/Input

(Optional) Offer **data, background information, or a specific example** the AI must work from. Paste text directly here rather than asking the AI to recall it.
